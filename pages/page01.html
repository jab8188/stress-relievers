Gardening


Do you have a green thumb?

Or maybe you know someone who can help you. Gardening is more than just planting flowers in a garden bed, or only growing seeds in early spring. Different vegetables and other flowers can be started indoors late winter so when that nice spring sun and showers appear your plants will be ready to be transplanted outside. Gardening is more than just planting seeds into soil. This gives people happiness, being able to nurture and care for something of your own and watch it grow.
