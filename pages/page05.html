Painting


Painting is another stress reliever because there is no limits to what or how you can paint. Some people will paint how they envision the world around them, while others will let painting be a way to let out their anger. Either way or in between the two, there is still no wrong way to paint anything. Fun part of painting too is, you do not have to use paint brushes and rollers, you can use your fingers, hands, toes, or feet if you really felt up to the task. You never know what you may come up with. So give any of these stress relievers a try!
